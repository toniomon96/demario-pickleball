# Venue Rules And Court Expansion

Use this when adding or reviewing a court path for `demariomontezpb.com`.

## Launch Routing Matrix

| Venue or path | Booking owner | Payment owner | Waiver / insurance owner | Site behavior |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Public outdoor courts | DeMario's site | DeMario | DeMario's site terms | Students can schedule the lesson time directly on the site. Mario confirms the exact court after booking. |
| Dallas Indoor Pickleball Club | PodPlay | PodPlay | PodPlay / venue | Route students to PodPlay to reserve the court, select DeMario, and complete required platform steps. |
| The Grove Pickleball | PodPlay | PodPlay | PodPlay / venue | Route students to The Grove's PodPlay flow. |
| Samuel-Grand Tennis Center | Impact Activities | Court through Impact; coaching fee to DeMario | Impact / venue plus DeMario's site terms when applicable | Student reserves the court through Impact, then coordinates the coaching fee and lesson details with Mario. |
| Life Time Fitness | Life Time | Life Time | Life Time | Active members book through Life Time's system. |
| TeachMe.To | TeachMe.To | TeachMe.To | TeachMe.To | Packages, payment, waiver, and marketplace protections stay on TeachMe.To. |

## Add-A-Court Checklist

Before adding a court, club, or platform to the public booking experience, capture:

- Venue name and public display name.
- Booking owner: DeMario's site, venue system, marketplace, or hybrid.
- Payment owner: DeMario, venue, marketplace, or split.
- Waiver and insurance owner.
- Whether direct scheduling through `demariomontezpb.com` is allowed.
- Court fees, membership requirements, and cancellation rules.
- Required booking link, app name, or student instructions.
- Written proof, policy page, agreement, or owner confirmation.
- Recommended site behavior: direct booking, platform card, by-request card, or do not list.

## Product Rule

Do not turn the booking modal into a directory of every possible court. Keep the main choice to:

- Outdoor public court
- Indoor / weather-proof
- Help me choose

Add detailed courts only when they change the student's next action. Otherwise, keep the detail in this matrix and let Mario confirm the exact court after booking or by text.
